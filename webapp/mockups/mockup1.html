How to use Frisbee Stats:

Teams Page:

Click on the Teams Page to view an alphabetical list of all D1 and D3 mens ultimate teams from the 24-25 season. Once on the Team Page, select any team to view their stats.

Leaderboard:

Click on the Leaderboard page to view the rankings of all Mens ultimate teams 24-25. Select your choice of ranking algorithm to view different rankings.

My Team is Better than Your Team (MTIBTYT):

Once on this page, select any two Mens teams from the drop down menus. If there is a sequence of teams for which team 1 beat team x who beat team y ... who beat team 2, this sequence will be outputted.
